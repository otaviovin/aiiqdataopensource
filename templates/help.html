Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I upload my spreadsheet with data?
Simply click the upload button on your data analysis dashboard and select your CSV file. Click on the button Start and our application will automatically process and analyze your data.
What file formats are supported?
We support CSV, Excel (xlsx, xls) file formats. All files must be under 20MB in size.

Features & Analysis

How does the AI analysis work?
Our AI system analyzes patterns in your data, generates insights, and provides recommendations based on industry best practices and historical trends.
Can I export the analysis results?
Yes, you can export results in PDF clicking in download button.
